## Seafood Dishes

### Lemon Herb Salmon with Roasted Vegetables

**Serves 4**

**Ingredients:**
- 4 salmon fillets (6 oz each)
- 2 zucchini, sliced
- 2 bell peppers, cut into strips
- 3 large carrots, cut into chunks
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 2 lemons (juiced and zested)
- 2 tsp dried oregano
- 1 tsp dried thyme
- Salt and pepper to taste

**Instructions:**
1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
2. Toss vegetables with half the olive oil, salt, and pepper. Spread on a large baking sheet.
3. Place salmon on the same sheet or a separate one. Brush with remaining olive oil.
4. Mix lemon juice, zest, oregano, and thyme. Pour over salmon and vegetables.
5. Roast for 15-20 minutes until salmon flakes easily and vegetables are tender.

### Mediterranean Tuna and Vegetable Pasta

**Serves 4**

**Ingredients:**
- 12 oz rice pasta or corn pasta
- 2 cans (5 oz each) tuna in olive oil
- 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
- 1/2 cup kalamata olives, pitted
- 2 tbsp capers
- 4 cups fresh spinach
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 2 lemons, juiced
- 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
- Salt and pepper to taste

**Instructions:**
1. Cook pasta according to package directions. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water before draining.
2. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
3. Add cherry tomatoes and cook for 3-4 minutes until softened.
4. Add tuna (with oil), olives, capers, and spinach. Cook until spinach wilts.
5. Add cooked pasta and toss. Add pasta water if needed for consistency.
6. Finish with lemon juice, basil, salt, and pepper.

### Herb-Crusted White Fish

**Serves 4**

**Ingredients:**
- 4 white fish fillets (cod or halibut, 6 oz each)
- 1 cup rice crackers, crushed
- 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
- 2 tbsp fresh dill, chopped
- 1 tbsp fresh chives, chopped
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- 3 medium potatoes, cubed
- 2 large carrots, cut into chunks
- Salt and pepper to taste

**Instructions:**
1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
2. Toss potatoes and carrots with 2 tbsp olive oil, salt, and pepper. Roast for 20 minutes.
3. Mix crushed crackers with herbs, remaining olive oil, and lemon juice.
4. Season fish with salt and pepper. Press herb mixture onto fish fillets.
5. Add fish to the pan with vegetables and roast for 12-15 minutes until fish flakes easily.

## Red Meat Dishes

### Greek-Style Lamb with Feta and Tomatoes

**Serves 4**

**Ingredients:**
- 8 lamb chops or 2 lbs leg of lamb, cut into portions
- 4 large tomatoes, cut into thick slices
- 6 oz feta cheese, crumbled
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 2 tbsp fresh oregano (or 1 tbsp dried)
- 1 tbsp fresh rosemary, chopped
- 2 lemons, juiced
- Salt and pepper to taste

**Instructions:**
1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
2. Season lamb with salt, pepper, oregano, and rosemary.
3. Heat 2 tbsp olive oil in an oven-safe pan over medium-high heat.
4. Sear lamb for 2-3 minutes per side until browned.
5. Arrange tomato slices around lamb, drizzle with remaining olive oil and lemon juice.
6. Transfer to oven and roast for 15-20 minutes for medium doneness.
7. Top with crumbled feta in the last 5 minutes of cooking.

### Italian Beef and Zucchini Bake

**Serves 6**

**Ingredients:**
- 2 lbs ground beef
- 4 large zucchini, sliced lengthwise into 1/4-inch strips
- 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
- 2 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
- 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- Salt and pepper to taste

**Instructions:**
1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. Brown ground beef in a large pan, breaking it up as it cooks. Season with salt, pepper, and oregano.
3. Add crushed tomatoes and simmer for 10 minutes. Stir in fresh basil.
4. Meanwhile, brush zucchini slices with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
5. Layer half the zucchini in a greased 9×13 baking dish.
6. Spread meat sauce over zucchini, then layer remaining zucchini on top.
7. Top with mozzarella and bake for 35-40 minutes until bubbly and golden.

### Steak with Herb Butter and Grilled Vegetables

**Serves 4**

**Ingredients:**
- 4 ribeye or sirloin steaks (8 oz each)
- 1 large eggplant, sliced into rounds
- 2 zucchini, sliced lengthwise
- 2 red bell peppers, cut into strips
- 1/2 cup butter, softened
- 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
- 2 tbsp fresh chives, chopped
- 1 lemon, zested
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper to taste

**Instructions:**
1. Mix softened butter with herbs and lemon zest. Set aside.
2. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
3. Brush vegetables with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
4. Grill vegetables for 4-5 minutes per side until tender with grill marks.
5. Season steaks with salt and pepper. Grill for 4-6 minutes per side for medium-rare.
6. Let steaks rest for 5 minutes, then top with herb butter before serving.

### Spanish-Style Beef and Potato Skillet

**Serves 4**

**Ingredients:**
- 2 lbs beef chuck, cut into 1-inch cubes
- 4 medium potatoes, cubed
- 2 bell peppers, cut into strips
- 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
- 4 oz manchego cheese, cubed
- 2 tsp smoked paprika
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- Salt and pepper to taste

**Instructions:**
1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Brown beef cubes on all sides, about 8 minutes total. Season with salt and pepper.
3. Add potatoes and cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
4. Add bell peppers, tomatoes, and paprika. Bring to a simmer.
5. Cover and cook for 25-30 minutes until beef is tender and potatoes are cooked through.
6. Stir in manchego cheese just before serving.

### Mediterranean Meatballs with Parmesan

**Serves 4-6**

**Ingredients:**
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1/2 lb ground pork
- 1/2 cup rice breadcrumbs (or finely ground rice crackers)
- 1 egg
- 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
- 1 tbsp fresh oregano
- 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
- 1/2 cup parmesan cheese, grated
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper to taste

**Instructions:**
1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
2. Mix ground meats, breadcrumbs, egg, parsley, oregano, salt, and pepper.
3. Form into 1.5-inch meatballs and place on a baking sheet.
4. Drizzle with olive oil and bake for 20 minutes.
5. Meanwhile, heat crushed tomatoes in a large pan and season with salt and pepper.
6. Add meatballs to the sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
7. Serve topped with grated parmesan.

### Pork Tenderloin with Olive Tapenade

**Serves 4**

**Ingredients:**
- 2 pork tenderloins (1 lb each)
- 1 cup mixed olives, pitted
- 2 tbsp capers
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 1 lemon, juiced
- 4 oz goat cheese, crumbled
- 3 medium carrots, cut into chunks
- 3 medium parsnips, cut into chunks
- Salt and pepper to taste

**Instructions:**
1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
2. For tapenade: pulse olives, capers, 2 tbsp olive oil, and lemon juice in a food processor until chunky.
3. Toss root vegetables with remaining olive oil, salt, and pepper. Roast for 20 minutes.
4. Season pork with salt and pepper. Sear in an oven-safe pan for 2-3 minutes per side.
5. Transfer to oven and roast for 15-20 minutes until internal temperature reaches 145°F.
6. Let rest 5 minutes, then slice and serve with tapenade and crumbled goat cheese.

## Chicken Dishes

### Chicken Thighs with Olives and Tomatoes

**Serves 4**

**Ingredients:**
- 8 bone-in chicken thighs
- 2 cups cherry tomatoes
- 1/2 cup kalamata olives, pitted
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 2 tbsp fresh oregano (or 1 tbsp dried)
- 1 lemon, juiced
- Salt and pepper to taste

**Instructions:**
1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. Season chicken thighs with salt, pepper, and oregano.
3. Heat olive oil in a large oven-safe pan over medium-high heat.
4. Brown chicken thighs skin-side down for 5-6 minutes until crispy.
5. Flip chicken and add tomatoes and olives around the pan.
6. Transfer to oven and roast for 25-30 minutes until chicken is cooked through.
7. Drizzle with lemon juice before serving.

### Mediterranean Chicken and Rice Skillet

**Serves 4**

**Ingredients:**
- 2 lbs chicken thighs, cut into pieces
- 1.5 cups arborio rice
- 3 cups chicken broth
- 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
- 1/2 tsp saffron (or 1 tsp turmeric)
- 4 oz feta cheese, crumbled
- 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper to taste

**Instructions:**
1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Brown chicken pieces and season with salt and pepper. Remove and set aside.
3. Add rice to the same pan and stir for 2 minutes until lightly toasted.
4. Add broth, tomatoes, and saffron. Bring to a boil.
5. Return chicken to pan, reduce heat to low, cover and simmer for 20 minutes.
6. Let stand 5 minutes, then top with feta and parsley.

### Grilled Chicken with Caprese Salad

**Serves 4**

**Ingredients:**
- 4 chicken breasts (6 oz each)
- 8 oz fresh mozzarella, sliced
- 4 large tomatoes, sliced
- 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
- Salt and pepper to taste

**Instructions:**
1. Season chicken with salt and pepper. Grill for 6-7 minutes per side until cooked through.
2. Let chicken rest for 5 minutes, then slice.
3. Arrange tomato and mozzarella slices on plates, alternating.
4. Add sliced chicken and fresh basil leaves.
5. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar just before serving.

### Chicken and Vegetable Casserole with Parmesan

**Serves 6**

**Ingredients:**
- 2 lbs chicken thighs, cut into pieces
- 3 zucchini, sliced
- 2 bell peppers, cut into strips
- 4 medium potatoes, cubed
- 1/2 cup parmesan cheese, grated
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 1 tbsp fresh rosemary, chopped

- Salt and pepper to taste

**Instructions:**
1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).

- 1 tsp smoked paprika

### Herb-Roasted Whole Chicken

**Serves 4-6**

**Ingredients:**
- 1 whole chicken (4-5 lbs)
- 2 lemons (1 juiced, 1 quartered)
- 2 tbsp fresh rosemary, chopped
- 1 tbsp fresh thyme
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 4 medium potatoes, halved
- 2 large carrots, cut into chunks
- Salt and pepper to taste

**Instructions:**
1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
2. Pat chicken dry and season inside and out with salt and pepper.
3. Stuff cavity with lemon quarters and half the herbs.
4. Rub skin with olive oil and remaining herbs.
5. Surround with vegetables and drizzle with lemon juice.
6. Roast for 60-75 minutes until internal temperature reaches 165°F.
7. Let rest 10 minutes before carving.

### Chicken Saltimbocca Style

**Serves 4**

**Ingredients:**
- 4 chicken breasts, pounded thin
- 8 thin slices prosciutto
- 8 fresh sage leaves
- 1/4 cup white wine
- 1/4 cup parmesan cheese, grated
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper to taste

**Instructions:**
1. Season chicken with salt and pepper.
2. Top each breast with 2 sage leaves and 2 slices prosciutto. Secure with toothpicks.
3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
4. Cook chicken prosciutto-side down for 4-5 minutes until crispy.
5. Flip and cook 3-4 minutes more until chicken is cooked through.
6. Add wine to deglaze pan, scraping up any browned bits.
7. Sprinkle with parmesan before serving.

## Vegetarian Sides & Salads

### Quinoa Tabbouleh (Modified)

**Serves 4-6**

**Ingredients:**
- 1 cup quinoa, cooked and cooled
- 2 cups cherry tomatoes, diced
- 1 large cucumber, diced
- 1 cup fresh parsley, chopped
- 1/2 cup fresh mint, chopped
- 3 green onions (green parts only), chopped
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 2 lemons, juiced
- Salt and pepper to taste

**Instructions:**
1. Cook quinoa according to package directions and let cool completely.
2. Combine quinoa with tomatoes, cucumber, herbs, and green onion tops.
3. Whisk together olive oil and lemon juice.
4. Toss salad with dressing and season with salt and pepper.
5. Chill for at least 30 minutes before serving.

### Grilled Eggplant and Red Pepper Salad

**Serves 4**

**Ingredients:**
- 2 large eggplants, sliced into rounds
- 3 red bell peppers
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
- 1/4 cup pine nuts
- 4 oz feta cheese, crumbled
- 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
- Salt and pepper to taste

**Instructions:**
1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
2. Brush eggplant slices with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
3. Grill whole peppers until charred all over, about 12 minutes total.
4. Grill eggplant for 4-5 minutes per side until tender.
5. Place peppers in a bowl and cover with plastic wrap for 10 minutes.
6. Peel, seed, and slice peppers into strips.
7. Arrange eggplant and peppers on a platter.
8. Drizzle with balsamic vinegar, sprinkle with pine nuts, feta, and basil.
